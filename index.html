MIT's combined EECS program along with its fast-paced, learn-by-doing approach to teaching meant that I was exposed to both the principles of software construction, and solid state circuits. I like writing code. Science and math are a part of my character, they give me a deep-seeded respect for all the knowledge reality had to offer.
